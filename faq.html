General

Do we need another maven-mail-plugin?

The writing of this plugin was started as a project to learn more about Maven and for personal use.

After a while I thought it might be handy for other people as well.

So basically it is your choice. ;)

Features

Why can't directories be attached?

Think of it. Would you really want to get mail with a couple of dozen attachments?

If you really need to send whole directories, you should have a look at the excellent Maven Assembly Plugin and send the generated (and compressed) assemblies.

Why is the mimetype for the body hardcoded as "text/plain"? or Why can't I send fancy HTML bodies?

Short answer: an HTML body holds few if any any additional info compared to a plain text mail. But it at least doubles the size of an e-mail.

Long answer: Please see George Dillon's excellent articles "7 reasons why HTML e-mail is EVIL!!!" and "HTML e-mail is STILL evil!!!"

Will you implement multipart/alternative messages?

Short answer: No, see above.

Long answer: It is not very likely. But one of the major goals of this project is to give back to the community. If someone comes up with a good reason, I'll propably do it.
